About Us

Welcome to South Indian Restaurant — your go-to destination for authentic and flavorful South Indian cuisine. We bring the essence of Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Telangana, and Andhra Pradesh to your plate.

From crispy dosas to rich gravies and aromatic biryanis, every dish is handcrafted with fresh ingredients and traditional techniques. Whether you're here for a light bite or a festive meal, you're in for a warm and satisfying experience.

Veg Menu

Our vegetarian menu is a celebration of South Indian flavors crafted with fresh vegetables, grains, pulses, and spices. From idlis to biryanis, every bite is authentic and wholesome.

Non-Veg Menu

Our non-vegetarian specials offer bold flavors, tender meats, and classic dishes like biryanis, gravies, and starters. Enjoy every spice-filled mouthful, rooted in South Indian tradition.
